Rudra's Masala Bread Recipe

Ingredients

For the Filling:

  • 2 Medium Size Potato 🥔
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped 🧅
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped 🍅
  • 1-2 green chilies (optional) 🌶️
  • 1/2 tsp chaat masala or lemon juice 🍋
  • 2 tbsp chopped Dhaniya 🌿
  • 1-2 tbsp oil/butter 🧈
  • Salt as per taste 🧂
Saare Cheezo ko ek bowl mein mix kar do

Outer layer ke liye

  • 4-5 bread slices (jada bhi le sakte ho as per bhookh)
  • Thoda paani (bread soft karne ke liye)
  • Butter ya ghee (shallow fry karne ke liye)
Pro Tip: Bread ke Kinare kaat ke halka geela kardo, flat press karo, stuffing daal ke tight roll bano. Paani se chipkao, aur fry karke khao 🤤 🤤
